All
	to- not occupied by ally
	doesn't put own king in check
	doesn't move past edge of board

King
	castle iff:
		king hasn't moved
		rook hasn't moved
		king not in check
		no squares between (inclusive) king and rook occupied
		no squares between (inclusive) king and rook controlled by opposition

Queen, Rook, Bishop
	doesn't pass any material
		ie. unlike knight, these can't jump over other pieces

Pawn
	push iff:
		to- not occupied by oppositon
	capture if:
		to- occupied by opposition
		to- was passed during opposition pawn double-push (en passant)
